Cardiology Department

About Our Cardiology Department

The Department of Cardiology at Nepal Health Hospital provides comprehensive cardiovascular care, combining state-of-the-art technology with compassionate, patient-centered approach. Our team of experienced cardiologists and cardiac surgeons specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart diseases.

We offer a full spectrum of cardiology services ranging from preventive cardiology and diagnostic imaging to complex interventional procedures and cardiac surgery.

Conditions We Treat

  • ✓ Coronary Artery Disease
  • ✓ Heart Valve Disorders
  • ✓ Heart Failure
  • ✓ Arrhythmias (Irregular Heartbeat)
  • ✓ Congenital Heart Defects
  • ✓ Peripheral Artery Disease
  • ✓ Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
  • ✓ Cholesterol Management

Our Services

❤️

Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive procedures to diagnose and treat cardiovascular conditions including angioplasty, stent placement, and cardiac catheterization.

🩺

Non-Invasive Cardiology

Diagnostic testing including echocardiography, stress tests, Holter monitoring, and cardiac MRI to evaluate heart function.

🔬

Preventive Cardiology

Risk assessment, lifestyle modification programs, and preventive strategies to reduce cardiovascular disease risk.

⚡

Electrophysiology

Specialized care for heart rhythm disorders including ablation procedures, pacemaker implantation, and defibrillator therapy.

💓

Heart Failure Clinic

Comprehensive management of heart failure patients including medication optimization, device therapy, and advanced therapies.

🏃‍♂️

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Supervised exercise programs, education, and counseling to help patients recover after heart events and improve cardiovascular health.
