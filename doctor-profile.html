About Dr. Ramesh Sharma

Dr. Ramesh Sharma is a highly acclaimed cardiologist with over 18 years of experience in interventional cardiology and preventive cardiac care. He graduated from Kathmandu University with his MBBS, completed his MD in Internal Medicine from Tribhuvan University, and obtained his DM in Cardiology from the prestigious All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Delhi.

Dr. Sharma specializes in complex coronary interventions, structural heart disease treatments, and preventive cardiology. He has performed over 5,000 angiographies and 3,000 angioplasties throughout his career. His approach combines cutting-edge medical technology with compassionate patient care, ensuring each patient receives personalized treatment plans.

    Diagnostic Angiography

    Coronary angiography to detect blockages in heart arteries using minimally invasive catheter techniques.

    🎈

    Angioplasty & Stenting

    Balloon angioplasty with stent placement to open blocked coronary arteries and restore blood flow.

    🫀

    Pacemaker Implantation

    Insertion of pacemakers to regulate heart rhythm in patients with arrhythmias or heart block.

    🩺

    Heart Failure Management

    Comprehensive care for patients with heart failure including medication optimization and device therapy.

    🫁

    Stress Testing

    Exercise and pharmacological stress tests to evaluate heart function under physical stress.

    📊

    Echocardiography

    Ultrasound imaging of the heart to assess structure, function, and blood flow.
