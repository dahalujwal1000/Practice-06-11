Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to fast before the health checkup? +
For most packages, 8-12 hours of fasting is required for accurate blood sugar and lipid profile results. Water is allowed during fasting. Specific instructions will be provided when you book your appointment.
How long does the health checkup take? +
The basic package takes approximately 2-3 hours, while the comprehensive and executive packages may take 4-5 hours depending on the tests included. We recommend scheduling your appointment for the morning to complete fasting requirements comfortably.
Can I customize the health package? +
Yes, you can customize your health package by adding specific tests from our add-on list. Our healthcare advisors can help you create a personalized package based on your age, gender, medical history, and specific health concerns.
When will I get my results? +
Most basic test results are available within 24 hours. Specialized tests may take 2-3 days. You will receive notification when your reports are ready for collection or can access them through our patient portal.
