About Us

Know more about our journey, vision, and commitment to healthcare excellence.

Our Journey

Founded in 1998 by a group of dedicated physicians, Nepal Health Hospital began as a modest 50-bed facility with a vision to provide quality healthcare to all Nepalis. Over the past 25 years, we have grown into a 350-bed multi-specialty tertiary care hospital, serving patients from across Nepal and neighboring countries.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, adoption of cutting-edge medical technology, and unwavering commitment to patient safety and satisfaction.

Our Journey Through Time

1998

Founded as a 50-bed facility by a group of dedicated physicians with a vision to provide quality healthcare to all Nepalis.

2005

Awarded NABH accreditation for meeting national healthcare quality standards.

2010

Expanded to 100-bed capacity with addition of new departments and diagnostic facilities.

2015

Launched telemedicine services to reach patients in remote areas of Nepal.

2020

Designated as COVID-19 treatment center with dedicated isolation wards and ICU facilities.

2024

Inaugurated new wing with advanced surgical suites and expanded patient capacity.
